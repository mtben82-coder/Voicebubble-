### 2. Using the Overlay

Once enabled, you'll see:

**On the Home Screen:**
- A green banner saying **"Overlay is active!"**
- You can **Enable/Disable** it anytime with the button

**In Other Apps:**
- Open any app (WhatsApp, Gmail, Instagram, etc.)
- When you tap a text field and **keyboard opens**
- You'll see the **VoiceBubble floating bubble** (purple/pink gradient)
- **Tap the bubble** to expand it

### 3. Recording from Overlay

When you tap the bubble:
1. **Expanded overlay popup appears**
2. **Tap & Hold** the microphone icon to record
3. Speak your message
4. **Release** to stop recording
5. Your speech appears as text
6. **Choose a style** (Magic, Email, Casual, etc.)
7. Tap **"Generate Text"**
8. AI rewrites your text
9. **Copy** and paste into your app!

## ðŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### Overlay Not Showing?

**Check Permission:**
1. Go to Android Settings
2. Apps > VoiceBubble
3. Display over other apps
4. Make sure it's **Enabled** âœ…

**Re-enable in App:**
1. Open VoiceBubble
2. Tap **"Enable"** on the home screen banner
3. Grant permission if asked

### Overlay Disappeared?

- **Check battery settings:** Some phones kill overlay apps
- **Disable battery optimization** for VoiceBubble
- **Re-enable overlay** from the home screen

### Can't Tap the Overlay?

- Make sure you're **tapping directly** on the bubble
- Try **long-press** if normal tap doesn't work
- **Disable and re-enable** from settings
